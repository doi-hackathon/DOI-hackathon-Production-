Challenge Instructions

Younger people can pass along COVID-19 to their families and neighbors if they are positive but feel fine, may not have symptoms and have not been tested. Your task is to produce a deliverable that gives insights to how COVID is impacting young people in Dallas. To help facilitate your project, mentors from the data industry in Dallas will offer ‘office hours’ as a resource to help you develop your work. You can sign up for a mentor by clicking the "Find a Mentor" button below. Go to the "Data-sets" portion of the page to find out more about the data you will be using to complete this challenge.
