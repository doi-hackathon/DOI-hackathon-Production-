Judging Instructions

Thank you for participating as a Judge for the COVID19 Data Challenge! As a judge you will be evaluating submissions to this challenge based on criteria provided below. You will read the instructions for the challenge as they were provided to the students as well as review the Data-sets they have used to come up with their solutions. Considering the instructions, data, and grade level of the student, you will provide a score for each criteria for each submission that will result in an aggregate score.

Description of Challenge

Younger people can pass along COVID-19 to their families and neighbors if they are positive but feel fine, may not have symptoms and have not been tested. The student's task is to produce a deliverable that gives insights to how COVID is impacting young people in Dallas. To help facilitate their project, mentors from the data industry in Dallas will offer ‘office hours’ as a resource to help them develop their work.
