Welcome to

S.C.A.N.!


S.C.A.N., is a support group for families of children with additional needs. We are families coming together to build a network of support and to share information. Giving is what we do. We as parents, grandparents, siblings and caretakers of a child with additional needs all recognize the many challenges we face each day. S.C.A.N. is comprised of individuals, their families, providers, teachers, and coaches. They assist in enriching, consoling, educating, sharing frustrations, achievements, and most of all, fellowship.


What S.C.A.N. is all about:

  • Advocacy
  • Education
  • Socialization
  • Community Exposure
  • Awareness
  • Resources
  • Tolerance

Would you like to be part of a group that shares your concerns, questions, and achievements?

  • Do you need someone in your corner?
  • Do you want others to understand your child’s diagnosis?
  • Do you want a place for your children to be accepted for who they are?
  • Community Exposure
  • Do you want your child to experience events in a safe, comfortable atmosphere?
  • Would you like to help spread the word about children like yours, with understanding?
  • Would you like to have helpful information and share what you know?
  • Do you want your child to be surrounded by those that understand?

S.C.A.N. members enjoy meetings, opportunities, and socialization. If you would like to attend a meeting, check out our Events Page for a listing of S.C.A.N.’s upcoming events and meetings. For more information, please contact S.C.A.N. – we would love to have you as part of the S.C.A.N. family.
